How to get a direct image URL for party logos:
1. Go to Google Images and find the party logo
2. Click the image to open it, then click "Open image in new tab"
3. Copy the URL from that tab's address bar — it should end in .png, .jpg, or .webp
⚠ Do NOT copy from the Google Search results page — those URLs won't work.
Tip: Wikipedia / Wikimedia Commons always has direct image URLs that work reliably.
